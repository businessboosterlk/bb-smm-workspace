Escalation Decision Helper
When something goes wrong — use this before you panic
1

Is the client account suspended, hacked, or deleted?

YES → Escalate IMMEDIATELY to PM + Shiara + Thulaib. Do not wait. Call, don't text.
NO → Go to step 2.

2

Is the client actively angry or threatening to cancel?

YES → Flag to PM within 30 minutes. Do not try to solve alone.
NO → Go to step 3.

3

Is it a negative public comment or review?

YES → Screenshot it. Hide if brand guidelines allow. Post a neutral acknowledgement. DM the person. Flag to PM within 1 hour.
NO → Go to step 4.

4

Is a deliverable going to miss its deadline?

YES → Flag to PM 24 hours BEFORE the deadline. Include: what's late, why, when delivered, what you need.
NO → Go to step 5.

5

Is the client asking for something outside scope?

YES → Do not say yes. Do not say no. Say: "Let me check with the team and get back to you today."
Then flag to PM.
NO → Probably not an escalation. Solve it and log in the pipeline.
